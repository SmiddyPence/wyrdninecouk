Small Business Innovation

Where the magic happens, what we love to do

Our Offering

Do you have a great idea for a business? Remember you don't have to be big to be successful, your idea can be small, or just allow you the freedom to do what you love.

Whatever your circumstance, we would love to chat with you about your technology needs. We offer a free consultation to understand your goals and requirements.

Individual Mentoring

For a one man or woman band, the cost of hiring technology people can be daunting. With this programme, we offer coaching and advice on how to build simple solutions yourself.

This would cover simple solutions like single page websites showcasing your business, or starting to sell products on Shopify.

After initial setup, we would aim to leave you with the tools you need to support your solution.

Duration - a couple of days.

Idea Prototyping

One of the most common ways for a great idea to get up and running is prototyping. A prototype is a great way for potential customers and investors to see what your product is about.

We won't lie, we love to prototype, as developers it is especially rewarding to starting from a few ideas on paper and making them a reality.

This programme aims to get your idea out in the wild and start soliciting feedback.

Duration - a couple of weeks.

Bespoke Solutions

In the case that you have already secured funding and are looking to create a production-ready system we can help also.

Duration - several months.

The cost of these services vary depending on your specific needs, please feel free to get in touch to set up a consultation. We're open-minded and will consider equity sharing partnerships in some instances.
